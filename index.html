Playable Prototype

Manage a cyberattack on a critical transport signalling system.

You are the project management team. Across four rounds, your team must balance cost, cyber risk, transport service continuity, public trust and regulatory compliance.

There is no perfect answer. The aim is to create the most balanced, defensible and realistic recovery strategy.

How it works

Choose one strategy per round. A random uncertainty event appears after Round 3.

Learning focus

Trade-offs, risk thinking, stakeholder communication and long-term resilience.

Scenario Briefing

Your transport network is under cyber pressure.

A metropolitan rail signalling and control system has been affected by a cyberattack. Some services are delayed, technical teams are still identifying the full extent of the breach, and public concern is increasing.

Your team is the appointed project management team. You are not only restoring a technical system. You must also manage safety, service continuity, regulatory reporting, stakeholder communication, vendor coordination and long-term resilience.
